The \{#if} section can be used to control the flow of your template. The simplest possible version accepts a single parameter and renders the content if the condition is not falsy. However, you can also use operators: \{#if item.age > 10}, multiple conditions: \{#if item.age > 10 && item.price > 500} and the precedence rules can be overridden by parentheses: \{#if (item.age > 10 || item.price > 500) && user.loggedIn}. You can also add any number of \{#else} blocks.
